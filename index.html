Tufts Computer Science Exchange

The Exchange is dedicated to promoting computer science education and fostering community within the department here at Tufts.

About

The Computer Science Exchange evolved out of Tufts' ACM (Association for Computing Machinery).
We are a student-run organization inspired by the idea of unlocking the potential of computer
science students. We believe that each student stands to learn from their classmates, and we
provide the opportunity and the context for that intellectual exchange to occur.

Feel free to subscribe to our e-list and check out our Facebook page. You can also get to know
the executive board or send us an email.
